How to Play

🃏 The Basics
1

Every card has a suit, a rarity, and an effect — boost your points, protect yourself, steal, or shake things up.

2

Cards sometimes get a special twist called a Modifier, shown as a small badge — it changes how strong the card is.

3

Tap the little "i" on any card, anywhere in the game, to see exactly what it does in plain language.

🤖 Battle vs Bot & ⚔️ Duel of Fates
1

You and your opponent each get a hand of 5 cards. You take turns playing one card at a time.

2

Choose wisely — hold a Shield until you sense a Steal coming, or save a Skip to stop a comeback.

3

Whoever has the higher score when both hands are empty wins the round. First to win enough rounds wins the match.

🕯️ Reading Circle
1

Everyone in the room draws a card together and reveals it at the same time — no one loses.

2

After each round, the group's cards combine into a shared Group Fortune — a fun sentence made just for that draw.

🎭 Party Prediction
1

Everyone secretly draws a fortune. One at a time, a fortune is shown to the group — but not whose it is.

2

Guess who it belongs to for points. Guess wrong, or if nobody guesses right, the mystery player gets a bonus!
